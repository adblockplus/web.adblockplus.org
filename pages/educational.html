{{ educational_page_heading-2 Make it Easy to Find Adblock Plus }}

{{ educational_page_card_1_p_1 In an effort to declutter the browser toolbar, Edge, Chrome, and other browser developers are “hiding” browser extensions after they’re installed. To ensure that Adblock Plus remains easy to find, you’ll want to pin it to your browser. When you pin an extension to your browser’s toolbar, you’ll be able to open it with one click whenever you need to. }}

{{ educational_page_card_1_p_2 Pinning Adblock Plus will give you quick access to our menu. When you open our menu, you can see how many ads you’re currently blocking along with how many you’ve blocked in total. }}

{{ educational_page_card_1_p_3 You can turn Adblock Plus on and off right from the menu, as well as block specific page elements you don’t want to see. Opening the menu is also the place to access your settings or report any issues. }}

{{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_1_topic To pin the Adblock Plus extension in Chrome: }}

  1. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_1_li_1 Click the puzzle icon in Chrome’s toolbar to access the Chrome Extensions Menu. }}
  2. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_1_li_2 Find the ABP icon in the list in the dropdown that appears. }}
  3. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_1_li_3 Click on the pin icon to add the extension to your toolbar. The pin icon will turn blue once the extension is successfully pinned to your toolbar. }}

{{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_2_topic To pin the Adblock Plus extension on Edge: }}

  1. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_2_li_1 Click the puzzle icon to open the Edge Extensions Menu. }}
  2. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_2_li_2 Find the ABP icon in the list in the dropdown that appears. }}
  3. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_2_li_3 Click on the view/hide icon (eye icon) to add the Adblock Plus to the toolbar. }}

{{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_3_topic To pin the Adblock Plus extension on Firefox: }}

  1. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_3_li_1 Click the puzzle icon in the upper right corner to open the Firefox Extensions list. }}
  2. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_3_li_2 Find the ABP icon in the list in the dropdown that appears. }}
  3. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_3_li_3 Click on the settings icon (gear) next to the Adblock Plus extension. }}
  4. {{ educational_page_card_1_accordion_3_li_4 Click Pin to Toolbar. }}

{{ educational_page_card_2_title Enable Adblock Plus for Private Browsing }}

{{ educational_page_card_2_p_1 Are you someone that uses their browser’s private browsing mode? In Google Chrome, this is called Incognito Mode. In Edge, it’s called InPrivate. And for Firefox, it’s just called private browsing. }}

{{ educational_page_card_2_p_2 If so, it’s important to know that your browser’s private browsing function typically disables browser extensions. This is because extensions are often made by third parties and often involve tracking your browser history. }}

{{ educational_page_card_2_p_3 If you want to use Adblock Plus while browsing in private mode, it’s easy to enable. }}

  1. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_1_li_1 Enter chrome://extensions in the address bar to show a list of all your extensions. }}
  2. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_1_li_2 Find Adblock Plus in the extensions list and click Details. }}
  3. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_1_li_3 Scroll down and click the toggle switch next to Allow in incognito. }}
  1. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_2_li_1 Enter edge://extensions in the address bar to show a list of all your extensions. }}
  2. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_2_li_2 Find Adblock Plus in the extensions list and click Details. }}
  3. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_2_li_3 Scroll down and click the toggle switch next to Allow in InPrivate. }}
  1. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_3_li_1 Enter about:addons in the address bar to show a list of all your extensions. }}
  2. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_3_li_2 On the left side menu, click Extensions. }}
  3. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_3_li_3 Find Adblock Plus in the extensions list and click the three dot menu. }}
  4. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_3_li_4 Select Manage. }}
  5. {{ educational_page_card_2_accordion_3_li_5 Scroll down to Run in Private Windows and select Allow. }}
