{{s7 Spread the word}}

{{s17 If Adblock Plus has improved your browsing experience, why not recommend it to your friends, colleagues or blog followers?}}

{{s19 There are many ways you can help our cause:}}

  • {{s20 Follow us on Facebook, Google+, Instagram, and Twitter.}}
  • {{s21 Share the installation link on your blog or on social networks.}}
  • {{s22 Discuss the benefits of installing Adblock Plus at your organization.}}
  • {{s23 Write an op/ed piece, or contact your local media, about Internet privacy and security issues.}}

{{s8 Report bugs}}

{{s25 If you think Adblock Plus isn't behaving the way it should, tell us.}} {{s26 You may encounter a situation that is new to us, therefore we rely on your bug report to fix the issue.}} {{s27 Please be sure to include all of the necessary information needed to reproduce the issue, such as: the browser and browser version, the version of Adblock Plus and the conditions in which the issue occurs.}} {{s28 Additionally, please be on the lookout for any follow-up questions we may have.}}

{{s14 Add or improve filters}}

{{s53 Adblock Plus is only as good as its filters.}} {{s54 Share your filters, suggest new ones or help communities of volunteer contributors to draft and update filter lists.}} {{s55 These filter lists need to be constantly maintained to block menaces like new tracking systems, malware threats and intrusive ads.}} {{ filter-requirements-link Click here for a list of requirements. }}

{{s56 If you come across a website on which an ad isn't blocked, or a filter list causes an issue, please report it.}}

{{Notice eyeo GmbH, the company behind Adblock Plus, has no right to, or control over, any filter list content other than the ABP Anti-Circumvention Filter List and Acceptable Ads.}}

{{s15 Evaluate acceptable ads}}

{{s61 Eyeo is the originator behind the Acceptable Ads initiative, which is aimed at supporting websites that choose to use nonintrusive ads.}} {{s62 Criteria defining nonintrusive, and therefore acceptable ads, has been established based on strict guidelines.}}

{{s63 We encourage users to join the ongoing discussion to determine which ads qualify as acceptable ads. Visit the forum to share your thoughts.}}
