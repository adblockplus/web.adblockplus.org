{{ internet-evolves As the internet evolves, traditional pop-up ads aren’t the only distractions you face as you browse the web. There are “sticky” videos that float on your screen and follow you as you browse. And there are many different types of pop-up notifications asking you to sign up for newsletters, share your location, and more. With Adblock Plus Premium, you can enjoy an internet experience with far fewer annoying pop-ups and distractions. You’ll block more ads, pop-ups, and distractions for less than $2 a month. }}

  • {{ hide-newsletter Hide newsletter pop-ups. Block annoying pop-ups inviting you to sign up for a website's newsletter. }}
  • {{ stop-floating-videos Stop floating videos. Stop most floating videos from playing automatically and following you around your screen. }}
  • {{ ignore-survey-requests Ignore survey requests. Automatically dismiss invitations from websites to participate in surveys. }}
  • {{ block-site-notifications Block site notifications. Many websites ask to track your location when you land on their site. Now we’ll automatically decline 'allow notifications' requests from the websites you visit. }}
  • {{ hide-cookie-notices Hide cookie consent notices. Soon you’ll be able to put an end to those annoying cookie consent banners used on most websites thanks to a new cookie blocking feature we’re finishing up. }}
