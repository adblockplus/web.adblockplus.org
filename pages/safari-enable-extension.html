Oops! It looks like the Safari extension isn’t enabled. Here’s how to set it up:

1. Tap icon on the left side of the search field
2. Tap "Manage Extensions"
3. Tap the toggle to turn on Adblock Plus and tap Done
4. Tap next to Adblock Plus
5. Tap "Always Allow"
6. Tap "Always Allow on Every Website"
