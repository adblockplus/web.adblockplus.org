title=FAQ - Troubleshooting

{{s1 I (accidentally) blocked something. How can I unblock it again?}}

{{s2 In Firefox:}}

  1. {{s3 Select Firefox >> Add-ons (for Mac OS X / Linux, select "Tools" from the menubar >> "Add-Ons"), this will open your Firefox Add-ons Manager.}}
  2. {{s4 Click on "Extensions", find Adblock Plus there, go to "Options" and then click on "Filter preferences...".}}
  3. {{s5 At the top you will see the "Custom filters" tab where you find your own filters in filter groups.}}
  4. {{s6 Right-click a group and choose "Show/hide filters" and this will show you the custom filters.}}
  5. {{s7 Remove the respective filters from the right window. This should unblock the desired element again.}}

{{s8 In Chrome:}}

  1. {{s9 Click the Chrome menu button, then go to "Tools" and choose "Extensions".}}
  2. {{s10 Find Adblock Plus there and click on "Options" under its description.}}
  3. {{s11 Go to the "Add your own filters" tab. Here you can remove the custom filters.}}

{{s12 In Opera:}}

  1. {{s13 Click the "Menu" button (for Mac OS X / Linux, "Tools"), select "Extensions" >> "Manage Extensions".}}
  2. {{s14 Find Adblock Plus there and click its small tool-icon on the right side and choose "Preferences".}}
  3. {{s11 Go to the "Add your own filters" tab. Here you can remove the custom filters.}}
